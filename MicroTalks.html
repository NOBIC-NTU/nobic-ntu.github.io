NOBIC μ-Talks

NOBIC invites you to a (few-)monthly (typically 1st Monday of a month at 3.30 PM) series of approx. 30-min Zoom talks on microscopy-related topics. Recordings of past talks will be available on YouTube, under μ-Talks playlist (subject to authorisation by the speaker). After each talk, there will be enough room for discussion; questions related to the topic as well as any other (microscopy-related) questions addressed to the speaker or to NOBIC team will be welcome. If you would like to propose a topic for a future μ-Talk and/or if you'd like to present one, feel free to drop us an email.
