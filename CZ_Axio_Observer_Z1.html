| Carl Zeiss Axio Observer Z1 – inverted microscope



A motorised inverted epifluorescence microscope with a motorised stage allowing multi-position time-lapse imaging or tile scans of large samples. A heated stage top chamber is available upon request.

Available techniques:

  • Widefield epifluorescence imaging
  • Transmitted light imaging (brightfield, phase contrast, DIC)

Objectives:

  • EC Plan-Neofluar 5x/0.16 dry, FWD 18.5 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • EC Plan-Neofluar 20x/0.5 dry, FWD 2.0 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • LD Plan-Neofluar 40x/0.6 dry, FWD 2.5-3.3 mm, CG 0-1.5 mm (Phase contrast, DIC)
  • LD Plan-Neofluar 63x/0.75 dry, FWD 1.2-2.2 mm, CG 0-1.5 mm (Phase contrast, DIC)
  • EC Plan-Neofluar 63x/1.25 oil, FWD 0.1 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • Plan-Neofluar 100x/1.3 oil, FWD 0.2 mm, CG 0.17 mm (Phase contrast)
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Tube lenses: 1x, 1.6x

Fluorescence filter sets:

  • Zeiss set 49 - DAPI (G 365; 395; 420-470)
  • Zeiss set 10 - FITC (450-490; 510; 515-565)
  • Zeiss set 46 - YFP (490-510; 515; 520-550)
  • Zeiss set 31 - AF 568 (550-580; 585; 590-650)
  • Zeiss set 50 - Cy5 (625-655; 660; 665-715)

Fluorescence excitation sources:

  • HXP 120 C mercury metal halide lamp (intensity at sample)

Detectors and cameras:

  • AxioCam MRm CCD camera, 1388x1040 pixels, 6.45 µm/pixel

Software:

  • ZEN 2 (blue)

Other features:

  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised filter cube turret
  • Motorised focus drive
  • Stage-top incubator (on request)
