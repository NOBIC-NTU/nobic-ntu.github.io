| 2-photon FLIM microscope FVMPE-RS



An upright 2-photon microscope equipped with a broadly tunable fs laser, resonant scanner and a module for fluorescence lifetime imaging.

Available techniques:

  • fast 2-photon excited florescence imaging (using a resonant scanner)
  • high-quality 2-photon excited florescence imaging (using a galvano scanner)
  • 2-photon excited fluorescence lifetime imaging (FLIM)
  • second harmonic generation (SHG) imaging
  • 1-photon optogentic stimulation (405 nm, 458 nm, 588 nm)

Objectives:

  • XLPLN25XWMP2 25x/1.05 water, FWD 2 mm, CG 0 - 0.23 mm, brain sample index mismatch correction collar
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Fluorescence excitation sources:

Coherent Chameleon Vision (680 nm and 1040 nm tuneable) fs-laser

Detectors and cameras:

Olympus internal detectors:

  • 2x multialkali photomultiplier tube
  • 2x GaAsP photomultiplier tube

External FLIM detector:

  • PMA hybrid 40 mod (PicoQuant)

Emission filters:

For Olympus internal detectors:

  • 495-540 nm
  • 575-645 nm
  • 387/11 (for SHG imaging at 774 nm excitation)

For external FLIM detector (only a single filter at a time):

  • 458/64
  • 482/36
  • 535/50
  • 580/70
  • 600/50

Software:

  • FluoView
  • SymPhoTime 64

Other features:

  • Resonant scanner for fast imaging
  • Time-correlated single photon couting (TCSPC) FLIM module (PicoQuant PicoHarp 300)
  • Piezo focus drive
  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised focus
