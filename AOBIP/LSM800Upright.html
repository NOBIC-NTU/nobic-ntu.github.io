| Upright Confocal LSM 800



Carl Zeiss LSM 800 is a laser scanning confocal microscope, which allows simultaneous imaging in two spectrally freely selectable detection channels. It is equipped with water dipping objectives suitable for example for zebrafish embryos imaging.

Available techniques:

  • 3-dimensional confocal imaging

Objectives:

  • EC Plan-Neofluar 5x/0.15 dry, FWD 13.6 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • EC Plan-Neofluar 10x/0.3 dry, FWD 5.2 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • W N-Achroplan 20x/0.5 water dipping, FWD 2.6 mm, CG -
  • W Plan-Apochromat 40x/1.0 water dipping, FWD 2.5 mm, CG -
  • W Plan-Apochromat 63x/1.0 water dipping, FWD 2.1 mm, CG -
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Excitation laser lines:

  • 405 nm
  • 488 nm
  • 561 nm
  • 640 nm

Dichroics and beamplitters:

  • MBS 405/488/555/639

Detectors and cameras:

  • 2x Multi-alkali photomultipliers
  • Trasmitted light PMT

Software:

  • ZEN Blue

Other features:

  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised focus drive
