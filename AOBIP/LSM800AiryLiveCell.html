| Inverted Live-Cell Confocal LSM 800 with Airyscan Super-Resolution



Carl Zeiss LSM 800 is a laser scanning confocal microscope equipped with two highly sensitive GaAsP PMTs for two spectrally freely selectable detection channels and a GaAsP Airy scan detector for super-resolution imaging. The microscope is housed in an enclosure with temperature, CO2 and humidity control for long-term live-cell imaging. Hardware autofocus ensures stable focusing during long-term imaging.

Available techniques:

  • 3-dimensional confocal imaging
  • Super-resolution imaging with Airyscan detector (1.7 x improved resolution in all directions)
  • Long-term time-lapse imaging of living cells (with hardware autofocus and environment control)

Objectives:

  • Plan Apochromat 10x/0.45 dry, FWD 2.0 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apochromat 20x/0.8 dry, FWD 0.55 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • LD-Plan Neofluar 40x/0.6 dry, FWD 3.3 - 2.5 mm, CG 0 - 1.5 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apochromat 40x/1.3 oil, FWD 0.21 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • C-Apochromat 63x/1.2 water, FWD 0.28 mm (for 0.17 mm CG), CG 0.14-0.19 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apochromat 63x/1.4 oil, FWD 0.19 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Excitation laser lines:

  • 405 nm
  • 488 nm
  • 561 nm
  • 640 nm

Dichroics and beamplitters:

  • MBS 405/488/555/639

Detectors and cameras:

  • 2x GaAsP photomultipliers
  • Airyscan GaAsP super-reolution detector, compatible with 63x objective
  • Trasmitted light PMT

Software:

  • ZEN Blue

Other features:

  • Heated enclosure with CO2 atmosphere control
  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised focus drive
  • Definite Focus.2 hardware autofocus
