| 2-photon microscope Nemo



A custom upright 2-photon microscope with two laser lines, either of which can be used for fast 2-photon excited fluorescence imaging or for optogenetic stimulation.

Available techniques:

  • fast 2-photon excited florescence imaging (using a resonant scanner)
  • 2-photon optogenetic stimulation
  • second harmonic generation (SHG) imaging

Objectives:

  • W Plan-Apochromat 20x/1.0 water, FWD 1.8 mm, CG 0 mm
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Fluorescence excitation sources:

SPARK Alcor Duo (920 nm and 1064 nm) fs-laser

Emission filters:

  • Detector 1: 520/70
  • Detector 2: 593/46

Detectors and cameras:

2x GaAsP photomultiplier tube (Hamamatsu)

Software:

  • ScanImage

Other features:

  • Resonant scanner for fast imaging
  • Galvano scanner for optogentic stimulation
  • Piezo focus drive
  • Manual stage
