| High-Content Widefield Microscope IN Cell Analyzer 2200



GE IN Cell Analyzer 2200 is a high throughput widefield fluorescence microscope with sensitive sCMOS camera and a broad range of fluorescence filters. Sample temperature, CO2 and humidity control allow long term live-cell experiments; hardware autofocus ensures reliable focusing during long-term experiments. The image acquisition software contains image deconvolution functionalities.

Available techniques:

  • Transmitted light imaging (brightfield, DIC, computational phase contrast - constructed from in-focus and 2 out-of-focus images)
  • Widefield multi-channel fluorescence imaging with a fast and sensitive camera
  • Long-term time-lapse imaging of living cells (with environment control and hardware autofocus)
  • Fast multi-well plate screening

Objectives:

  • Plan Apo 4x/0.2 dry, FWD 15.7 mm, CG -
  • Plan Apo 10x/0.45 dry, FWD 4.0 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Fluor ELWD 20x/0.45 dry, FWD 8.1 - 7.0 mm, CG 0 - 2 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apo 40x/0.95 dry, FWD 0.14 mm, CG 0.11 - 0.23 mm (DIC)
    [FWD = working distance, CG = cover glass]

Light sources:

  • LED lamp (fluorescence)
  • LED lamp (transmitted light)

Filter sets:

Excitation filters:

  • CFP (458/24)
  • FITC (475/28)

Dichroic beamsplitters:

  • Quad-band (DAPI + FITC + Cy3 + Cy5)
  • Quad-band (DAPI + FITC + TexasRed + Cy5)
  • Quad-band (CFP + YFP + TexasRed + Cy5)

Emission filters:

Detectors and cameras:

Software:

Other features:

  • Motorised filter turret
  • Temperature and CO2 concentration control
  • Hardware autofocus
