| Inverted Confocal LSM 800 with Airyscan Super-Resolution



Carl Zeiss LSM 800 is a laser scanning confocal microscope equipped with two highly sensitive GaAsP PMTs for two spectrally freely selectable detection channels and a GaAsP Airy scan detector for super-resolution imaging.

Available techniques:

  • 3-dimensional confocal imaging
  • Super-resolution imaging with Airyscan detector (1.7 x improved resolution in all directions)

Objectives:

  • EC Plan-Neofluar 10x/0.3 dry, FWD 5.2 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • EC Plan-Neofluar 20x/0.5 dry, FWD 2.0 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apochromat 20x/0.8 dry, FWD 0.55 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • Plan Apochromat 40x/1.3 oil, FWD 0.21 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
  • Plan Apochromat 63x/1.4 oil, FWD 0.19 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Excitation laser lines:

  • 405 nm
  • 488 nm
  • 561 nm
  • 640 nm

Dichroics and beamplitters:

  • MBS 405/488/555/639

Detectors and cameras:

  • 2x GaAsP photomultipliers
  • Airyscan GaAsP super-reolution detector, compatible with 63x objective
  • Trasmitted light PMT

Software:

  • ZEN Blue

Other features:

  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised focus drive
