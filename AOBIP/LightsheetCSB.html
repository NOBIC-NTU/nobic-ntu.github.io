| Lightsheet microscope - Carl Zeiss Z.1



This lightsheet microscope is equipped with pivot scan for 3D imaging, a sensitive sCMOS camera and sample temperature control. Beside optics for imaging specimens in water, optics for imaging cleared specimens of refractive index n = 1.45 are also available. A camera and an IR LED (to minimise phototoxicity) in the sample chambre facilitate sample positioning.

Objectives:

Excitation (lightsheet formation):

  • 5x/0.1
  • 10x/0.2

Imaging:

Note that only a single objective lens can be installed in the microscope at a time.

  • EC Plan-Neofluar 5x/0.16, WD 5.1 mm, for water immersion
  • W Plan-Apochromat 20x/1.0 water immersion, WD 2.4 mm
  • Clr Plan-Neofluar 20x/1.0 Corr n = 1.45 clearing immersion, WD 5.6 mm
    [WD = working distance]

Fluorescence excitation lasers:

  • 445 nm
  • 488 nm
  • 515 nm
  • 561 nm
  • 638 nm

Fluorescence filters:

Note that only a single filter turret can be installed in the microscope at a time.

Emission filters turret 1:

  • CFP (BP 460-500)
  • GFP (BP 505-545)
  • Cy3 (BP 575-615)

Emission filters turret 2:

  • YFP (BP 525-565)
  • mCherry (LP 585)
  • DRAQ5 (LP 660)
    [BP = band pass, LP = long pass]

Detectors and cameras:

  • PCO.edge (1920x1920 pixels, 6.5 µm/pixel)

Software:

  • ZEN Blue

Other features:

  • temperature control
  • chambre for imaging cleared specimen with refractive index n = 1.45
