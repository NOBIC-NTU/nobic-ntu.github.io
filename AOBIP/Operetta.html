| High-Content Microscope Operetta CLS



Perkin-Elmer Operetta CLS is a high throughput confocal (spinning disk) and widefield fluorescence microscope with sensitive sCMOS camera, a broad range of fluorescence filters and 8 LED light sources covering the whole visible spectrum. Sample environment control (temperature, carbon dioxide and humidity) and automated immersion water delivery to water immersion objectives allow long term live-cell experiments. Hardware autofocus ensures stable focusing throughout long-term experiments.

Available techniques:

  • Transmitted light imaging (brightfield, computational phase contrast - constructed 2 images acquired at different axial position)
  • Widefield multi-channel fluorescence imaging with a fast and sensitive camera
  • Confocal (spinning disk) imaging
  • Long-term time-lapse imaging of living cells (with environment control and hardware autofocus)
  • Fast multi-well plate screening

Objectives:

  • Plan-Apochromat 5x/0.16 dry, FWD 12.1 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • EC Plan-Neofluar 10x/0.3 dry, FWD 5.2 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • LD Plan-Neofluar 20x/0.4 dry, FWD 8.4 - 7.4 mm, CG 0 - 1.5 mm
  • W Plan-Apochromat 20x/1.0 water, FWD 1.7 mm, CG 0.17 mm
  • LD C-Apochromat 40x/1.1 water, FWD 0.62 mm, CG 0.14 - 0.19 mm
  • LD C-Apochromat 63x/1.15 water, FWD 0.6 mm, CG 0.14 - 0.19 mm
    [FWD = working distance, CG = cover glass]

Fluorescence excitation sources:

  • LEDs:
    •  355-385 nm
    •  390-420 nm
    •  435-460 nm
    •  460-490 nm
    •  490-515 nm
    •  530-560 nm
    •  615-645 nm
    •  650-675 nm

Transmitted light source:

  • IR LED (740 nm) for low phototoxicity

Filter sets:

Emission filters:

  • DAPI (430-500 nm)
  • CFP (470-515 nm)
  • GFP (500-550 nm)
  • YFP (525-580 nm)
  • dsRed (570-650 nm)
  • DRAQ5 (655-760 nm)

Detectors and cameras:

  • sCMOS monochrome camera (2160x2160 pixels, 6.5 µm/pixel)

Software:

  • Harmony
  • Columbus (cloud-based analysis)

Other features:

  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised focus drive
  • Motorised filter turret
  • Temperature (to 42°C) and CO2 concentration control
  • Hardware autofocus
