| 2-photon microscope Guss



A custom upright 2-photon microscope with a single laser line for 2-photon excited fluorescence imaging and second harmonic generation imaging.

Available techniques:

  • 2-photon excited florescence imaging
  • second harmonic generation (SHG) imaging

Objectives:

  • C-Apochromat 40x/1.2 water, FWD 0.28 mm, CG 0.13-0.2 mm
    [FWD = free working distance, CG = cover glass]

Fluorescence excitation sources:

Coherent Axon 780 nm fs-laser

Emission filters:

  • Detector 1: 387/11
  • Beamsplitter: 495 LP
  • Detector 2: 642/80

Detectors and cameras:

2x Silicon photomultiplier (Hamamatsu)

Software:

  • ScanImage

Other features:

  • Manual stage
  • Piezo focus drive
